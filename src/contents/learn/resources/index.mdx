## Resources
Sports betting is filled with numbers, statistics, and "lines". Professionals must take
advantage of "weak lines" using a combination of experience and research. Luckily, the
community of online betting has grown considerably and amassed countless articles documenting
their wins and losses. Visit some of the resources listed below if you're interested in learning
how the pros think.
